▸ Reference & method notes

Geometry only. Each wire is drawn at its finished outer diameter (conductor plus insulation) taken from TE Connectivity control drawings. 55A (55A0111 / M22759/32, tin-coated copper, ETFE, 150°C) and 55M (55M0414, silver-plated alloy, ETFE, 200°C). Wires are packed by a deterministic nearest-fit routine and the bundle diameter is the smallest circle that encloses every wire. Real looms settle differently under tension and tape, so treat this as a tidy-pack estimate, not a guaranteed dimension. No sleeving allowance is included in this lite version.

Fill is the summed wire OD area divided by the enclosing-circle area. It's a rough measure of how tightly the bundle packs.

Sources: finished-wire diameters from TE Connectivity control drawings (55A0111, 55M0414).

Guidance only. Verify every bundle dimension against real parts and the applicable standards before committing to a harness build. If in doubt, build a test piece. Full Boost Consulting Ltd accept no liability for reliance on these outputs.
